# pm-bench daily-report notes — 2026-05-07

## Observations

- **The universe got more concentrated, not bigger.** Total IO-addressable markets fell 2,634 → 2,549 (−3.2%), but unique IO domains dropped further: 32 → 26 (−18.8%). Six distinct sources disappeared while the surviving ones absorbed the share. Twitch alone went from 24.7% to 29.1% of IO (+4.4pp). On a "reach" basis IO sees fewer kinds of places this morning, even though the headline market count looks similar.
- **The "empty source" pile collapsed: 379 → 69 markets** (Gate 1b drop fell from 6.6% to 1.3% of all rows). That's the single biggest structural change in the snapshot. Either Polymarket populated `eventResolutionSource` on a large slice of UMA markets overnight, or the prior day's empty-source markets were predominantly in the Election/political family that aged out (which is also gone today, 70 → 0). Both explanations need a third day of data to disambiguate.
- **Templates +5, markets −85, recurring share +2.2pp.** The same set of templates is producing fewer market instances per template — the recurring fan-out is shrinking even as recurring's share of IO grew (28.6% → 30.8%). The big single-fixture fan-outs are still doing the work: `Cavaliers vs. Pistons` (37 markets), `Aston Villa vs. Nottingham Forest` (34), `SC Freiburg vs. SC Braga` (34) — each one fixture, dozens of prop sub-markets.
- **News / other doubled in market count (130 → 258, +128) on +7 templates.** Most of the growth is concentrated in one template: `## Free App in the US Apple App Store on May #?` is alone at 42 markets, all with empty source. This is currently classified as News/other but its real shape is "ranked-list-on-a-day" — a category the classifier doesn't yet have. Worth a `categorize.ts` rule.
- **Sports match dropped harder than the markets number suggests.** −9 templates (37 → 28), so we lost nearly a quarter of distinct sports fixtures. The −93 markets is the survivors fanning out wide. The CONMEBOL stack (`conmebollibertadores.com` + `www.conmebol.com`) split across two domains still totals 108 markets / 4.2% of IO — one tournament accounting for ≈4% of IO's reach is a clustering opportunity if we ever care about per-tournament uniqueness.
- **The deterministic-feed kinds are quietly growing.** Stock close threshold templates jumped 28 → 44 (+16), markets +16 — 1 market per new template, the daily strike fan-out. Crypto price feed unchanged (49 templates / 270 markets). Together these are 322 markets (12.6% of IO) that the truly-IO-shaped count correctly excludes; worth noting that "IO-addressable" still includes them and the gap to "truly-IO-shaped" widened today.

## Anomalies / things to verify

- **`(empty)` is the 3rd-largest "domain" inside IO at 13.4%** (342 markets), even though Gate 1b only drops 69. The difference is markets where `eventResolutionSource` is empty *but* `description` carries a URL. The deterministic report counts these as IO-addressable; the practical question for Gate 2 is whether description-only URLs are reliable enough to act on.
- **`www.kick.com` (33 markets at 1.3% yesterday) dropped to 0**, but `kick.com` (no www) is at 284 markets / 11.1% — most likely the same site, two casing variants. Classifier already treats both as `streamer_or_view_count` per `categorize.ts` STREAMER_DOMAINS; the bucket merge for the report would clean up the noise.

## Recommended actions

- **Add a "ranked-list / app-chart" kind to `categorize.ts`** — the `## Free App in the US Apple App Store` template is 42 markets and empty-source, classified as News/other. A targeted rule (`/free app|top app|chart on may/i`) gives this its own bucket and stops it from puffing up News/other every day.
- **Normalize the `kick.com` / `www.kick.com` split in domain buckets.** A small `domainOf` post-pass that strips a leading `www.` for known equivalence pairs would make the daily-report's "dropped domain" warnings less noisy.
- **Hold off on a Gate 1 rule for `(empty)` until we see a third day.** The 5.3pp drop in no-URL is too big to be noise but also too clean to be only the political-cycle aging out. One more poll cycle resolves whether to invest in description-URL extraction as a Gate 2 path.
